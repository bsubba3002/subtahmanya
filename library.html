Library

The VCET Library is housed in a spacious new building Krishna Chetana(ground floor). The library with a floor area of 877 sq meters. Segregated in to various sections like Lending, Reference, Digital library, Newspaper, Periodicals, Reading room etc.
Digital Library Digital library having 12 systems with high speed internet facility. The digital Library exclusively used for the online access of e-Journals, e-books, conference proceedings, articles, educational videos and e-resources.
Library Rules Maintain silence in the library.
Check- In and Check -out is must to use the library resources..
Personal belongings like bag, personal books, and umbrella etc. are not allowed in to the library..
Student ID card is not transferable. The borrower is responsible for the books borrowed on his/her card..
Books will be issued after scanning the student ID card for 14 days only..
Students are required to check the books while borrowing and they will be responsible for any type of damage noticed at the time of return..
Overdue Charges- First 30days after the due date Rs.1 per day, After 30days Rs.2 per day. .
Members must replace the book if lost with latest edition along with overdue charges..
No discussion is permitted inside the library..
Refreshment of any kind shall not be taken in to the library..
Produce ID card whenever no due certificate is to be collected from the library and there should not be any dues on borrowed books.
