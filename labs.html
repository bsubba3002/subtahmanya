IoT lab Internet of Things (IoT) is an ecosystem of connected physical objects that are accessible through the internet. IoT is the mixture of hardware, software, data, and services. It covers a huge scope for industries and applications.

Our ICIoT Lab intends to provide end to end infrastructure which enables the students to transform their ideas into socially relevant projects. The state-of-the-art laboratory helps researchers as well as potential entrepreneurs to explore the commercial angle of their work.
